Rendang is an Indonesian spicy meat dish originating from the Minangkabau region in West Sumatra, Indonesia. Rendang is a piece of meat most commonly beef that has been slow cooked and braised in a coconut milk and spice mixture, well until the liquids evaporate and the meat turns dark brown and tender, becoming caramelized and infused with rich spices.
